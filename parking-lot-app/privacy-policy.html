Privacy policy

This page describes how the Parking Lot app handles data. Last updated: March 2026.

What we process

  • On your device: Tasks, journal entries, relationship notes, habits, and preferences are stored in browser storage (e.g. localStorage). Clearing site data or using a different browser profile removes or isolates that copy.
  • With Supabase (optional): If you enable sync or pair mode, selected data is stored in your Supabase project: device preferences, paired “Talk about” items, optional email-triage tasks, push subscription endpoints, and related metadata. Your project’s region and retention follow your Supabase settings.
  • Hosting: The static app may be served from GitHub Pages or another host; that host sees normal HTTPS requests (URLs, timing, IP) but not the contents of your encrypted traffic beyond what the platform logs.

Subprocessors

  • Supabase — database, realtime, and related APIs you configure.
  • GitHub Pages (or your chosen static host) — delivery of HTML, JS, and CSS.
  • jsDelivr / unpkg — CDN scripts referenced from index.html (Supabase client, chrono-node) load from those networks when the app runs.

Export & deletion

You can export a JSON backup from Settings. To delete cloud-side rows, use Supabase tooling or contact whoever operates the project. We do not operate a central account system inside the app.

Contact

For privacy questions about a deployment you use, contact the person or organization that gave you access to that instance and their Supabase project.
